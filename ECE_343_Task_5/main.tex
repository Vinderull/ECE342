        \begin{abstract}
        
        The design, simulation and construction of a differential amplifier circuit (developed in task 4) with feedback is described. In this task a multistage op-amp with a class B output amplifier will be developed, simulated and constructed. The required differential voltage gain for this circuit is 200 $\frac{V}{V}$ while driving the smallest load possible. The uncompensated unity gain is required to be larger than 150 kHz.  The gain was measured at 63.3 dB while unloaded. The smallest value for the load resistor which caused a 3 dB drop in gain was found to be 500 $\Omega$, with a gain measured at 66.33 dB. The unity gain uncompensated unity gain frequency was measured at 170 kHz.         
        

        \end{abstract}
